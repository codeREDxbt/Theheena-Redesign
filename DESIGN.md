# Design System: Heena — Personal Brand Redesign
**Project ID:** 4618142821386663405

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The overall atmosphere of the "Heena — Personal Brand Redesign" is "Editorial, Minimal, and Authoritative." It relies on a "less is more" aesthetic where negative space is abundant, giving the content a sophisticated, gallery-like presentation. The interface balances sharp typography with highly subtle color palettes to emphasize professionalism, trust, and refinement.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Deep Charcoal** (`#1A1A1A`): Primary text color and background for high-contrast sections (like the Footer and Video Testimonials).
* **Warm Ivory / Off-White** (`#F5F0E8`): The primary background color for light sections, creating a softer, more luxurious feel than stark white.
* **Terracotta / Signature Orange** (`#C3612C` / `#C4622D`): The primary accent color used for subtle highlights, active states, buttons, and important textual accents.
* **Pure White** (`#FFFFFF`): Used occasionally for contrasting cards on the Ivory background or for text overlaid on Deep Charcoal.
* **Muted Grey/Border** (`#E2DDD6`): Used for subtle dividing lines and structural borders.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Primary Font Family:** Manrope (as specified by Stitch, though often paired with Cormorant Garamond for display in editorial layouts).
* **Headings:** Extra-bold, high-impact display typography (`Cormorant Garamond` typically) for primary headers, creating a magazine-like structure.
* **Body:** Clean, highly legible sans-serif (`Manrope` or `DM Sans`), typically in Regular weight with generous line height (1.7) for readability.
* **Labels:** Small, uppercase, highly tracked text (e.g., 11px with 0.12em+ letter spacing) used for section eyebrows, dates, and metadata to reinforce luxury.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Solid Terracotta background with sharp or subtly rounded edges (`roundness: ROUND_FOUR`). Minimalist hover states without heavy shadows. Ghost buttons rely on text color and subtle underlines rather than bounding boxes.
* **Cards/Containers:** Subtly rounded corners (`4px` radius). Cards either blend into the background with just a border stroke or use stark contrast (Deep Charcoal cards on Ivory) to command attention. Shadows are virtually non-existent, preferring flat, modern borders.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Clean, single stroke borders with minimalist padding. 

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace & Grid:** Layouts are heavily constrained to a centered grid (typical max-width 1280px) with substantial breathing room (80px+ padding) between the content and screen edges. 
* **Alignment:** Items typically align left or center strongly on an underlying column structure. The design avoids edge-to-edge stretching of text to preserve readability.
